Who we are

An agency built on proof, not promises.

Klix Digital started with a simple frustration: too many agencies sell hours, not outcomes. We built the opposite — a lean team obsessed with data, speed and honest reporting.

Our story

Founded by marketers who were tired of the smoke and mirrors.

We started Klix Digital in Cape Town after years spent inside agencies that optimised for retainers, not results. We wanted to build something different: a team small enough to move fast, senior enough to get it right the first time, and transparent enough that clients never have to wonder what they're paying for.

Today we work with e-commerce brands, hospitality groups, fintechs and local SMEs across South Africa — all held to the same standard: if a channel isn't producing, we say so and change course.

What we believe

Our values, in practice.

01

Data over opinion

Every recommendation is tied to a number. If we can't measure it, we don't pitch it.

02

Speed is a feature

Campaigns launch in days, not months. Momentum compounds — delay costs more than it saves.

03

Radical transparency

You get access to live dashboards, not a slide deck once a month.

04

Long-term thinking

We turn down quick wins that damage brand equity. Growth has to compound, not just spike.
