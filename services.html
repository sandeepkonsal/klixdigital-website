FAQ

Common questions.

What's the minimum contract length?+

Most engagements run month-to-month after an initial 3-month period, which is how long most channels need to show statistically meaningful results.

Do you work with businesses outside South Africa?+

Yes — while most of our clients are South African, we run campaigns for brands across Africa, the UK and the Middle East.

Can I combine services from different packages?+

Absolutely. The packages are a starting point — every proposal is scoped around your specific goals and budget.

How is ad spend billed?+

Ad spend is billed separately, directly through your own ad accounts, so you always retain full ownership and visibility.
